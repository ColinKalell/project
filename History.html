Colin's Golf Lessons

My involvement in the game began when I was 12 years old and needed a summer job. My neighbor recommended that I go to the local private country club to get a job as a caddie. I knew nothing about golf, and found it to be rather boring and pedestrian. I enrolled in caddie training classes, and completed the required 3 classes so that I could begin my job as a caddie. I began caddying that summer, and quickly took a liking to the game. There are many aspects of the game that drew me in, but the camaraderie and competitive nature was the main allure. Fast forward 20+ years, and I’m still involved in the game. I have caddied for some of the most affluent, and influential people in the world. I have caddied for professionals, as well as amateurs. I have brought my knowledge of the game to help them play to the best of their ability while on the course with me. I have seen thousands of swings over the years, from great players to beginners in the earliest stages. I have seen golfers shoot under par, and I have seen golfers shoot 150 and everywhere in between. I will use my knowledge of the game to breakdown a few swings on my “Check Out The Action” page.
